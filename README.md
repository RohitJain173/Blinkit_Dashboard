# 🛒 Blinkit Customer Behaviour Analysis
## 📌 Project Description

This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior on a quick commerce platform (Blinkit) to identify buying patterns, customer segments and key revenue drivers. The dashboard provides insights into how factors like delivery speed, discounts, product categories and customer preferences influence purchasing decisions and overall business performance.

## ❗Business Problem
- Low customer retention in quick commerce environment
- Ineffective discount and promotional strategies
- Lack of clarity on high-demand products and categories
- Limited understanding of customer purchasing behavior
  
## 🎯Key Objectives
- Identify high-value and repeat customers
- Analyze the impact of discounts on order value
- Determine top-performing product categories
- Understand customer behavior across different time slots and seasons
- Improve retention and marketing effectiveness
  
## 🛠Tech Skills

- Python → Data Cleaning & EDA (Pandas, NumPy)
- SQL → Data Analysis & Querying
- Power BI → Dashboard & Data Visualization
  
## 📊Dataset

Download the dataset [Link](https://github.com/RohitJain173/Blinkit_Dashboard/blob/main/BlinkIT%20Grocery%20Data.xlsx)

The dataset simulates Blinkit-like quick commerce transactions and includes:

- Customer ID
- Age, Gender
- Product Category (Groceries, Snacks, Beverages, etc.)
- Order Value
- Delivery Time
- Discount Applied
- Payment Method
- Order Time (Morning, Evening, Night)
- Customer Rating
  
## 🚀Key Features

- 👥 Customer Segmentation (Repeat vs New Customers)
- 🛒 Category-wise Revenue Analysis
- 💸 Discount Impact on Order Value
- ⏱️ Delivery Time Analysis
- 🕒 Time Slot-based Order Trends
- 💳 Payment Method Distribution
- ⭐ Customer Rating Insights

## 📊Key Insights

- 🛒 Groceries and daily essentials generate the highest revenue
- 💸 Orders with discounts show higher average order value
- 👥 Repeat customers contribute significantly to total revenue
- ⏱️ Faster delivery times improve customer satisfaction and retention
- 💳 Digital payments (UPI/Online) are the most preferred
- 🕒 Evening and night time slots show higher order volume
